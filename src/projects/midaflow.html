What is enterprise resource planning?

Enterprise resource planning (ERP) is a comprehensive software system designed to integrate and streamline various aspects of an organization's core business activities. It serves as a centralized hub that connects different departments and functions within the organization such as:

The founders of Midaflow saw a gap in the market. Predominantly, ERP software systems were only accessible to large organizations with the resources to afford such a comprehensive enterprise solution. They believed that ERP software systems should be more accessible and easy-to-use for small and medium-sized businesses. For Midaflow, they focused their efforts on the financial aspects of an ERP software system, specifically focusing on Finance & Accounting, Analytics & Reporting, and Purchasing.
